Mera dost hai ek

Jo kabhi chhoti, to kabhi badi behen ban jaati hai,
Kabhi best-friend to kabhi Bhai ban jaati hai,
Aise to khoob samajhti hai ye hamko,
Lekin jab kuch pucho to kehti hai- "Abhi theek se jaanti hu main tumko".

Nahi hai pasand ise jyada bolna,
Ya ye sirf mere saath hi aisi hai, kyunki
Puri kahani bhi suna do to kehti- "Sahi hai".

Hai mera ek dost
Jise yaad dilana padta hai ki main bhi karta hu exist,
Jise chahiye galat kaamo mein mujhse assist,
Lekin jab kar du main resist, koi farak nahi padta ise, aur
Kartii hai ye phir bhi persist.

Hai mera ek dost
 Thodi Moti hai,
Thodi na samajh hai,
But, "Sahi hai",
Dusron ki zindagi dekh kar sochti hai,
"Main in jaisa kyun nahi?"
Kyunki thodi apni zindagi se pareshan hai,
Thodi nadaan hai.
Par mere dost, ek baat samjh lena tum,
Takleef ke toofan se jo guzra hai,
Uss hi ke haathon meetha phal nazar aaya hai.
